Welcome to the KPI Dashboard!

question_answerWhat is it?

KPI Dashboard is a web application tool that allows Scrum Members (specifically Scrum Masters) to track, check and analyze how the project in progress is going through user-friendly Dashboards.

question_answerHow is it done?

Through Key Performance Indicators (KPI). KPIs are metrics and indicators that give information about an specific key-factor in project developing. This application is KPI Scrum Metrics oriented, due to it's capacity in tracking and measuring Scrum development metrics.

question_answerHow many KPIs are tracked?

Over 10. They are carefully accurate and under analysis. We have decided to divide them into categories according to what they measure and to have data more organised.

categoryThe Dashboard categories

We classified similar KPIs into Dashboards, making a total of 4 Dashboards. The Scrum Team Management Dashboard, the Sprint Management Dashboard, the Efectiveness Management Dashboard and the Bug Management Dashboard
